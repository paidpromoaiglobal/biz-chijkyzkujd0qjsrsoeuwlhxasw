About Us

ABRA Dental Clinic & ESTHETIC DENTAL LAB

ABRA Dental Clinic & Esthetic Dental Lab is a trusted dental care destination located in the heart of Ariyamangalam, Kamaraj Nagar, Tiruchirappalli, Tamil Nadu. With a stellar rating of 4.8 out of 5 from over 125 satisfied patients, the clinic is renowned for combining advanced dental treatments with a warm, patient-first approach. Led by Dr. Mohamed Yaseen and a dedicated team of dental professionals, ABRA offers a calm, welcoming environment that puts even the most anxious patients at ease.

From routine check-ups and root canal procedures to esthetic dental lab services, ABRA Dental Clinic is equipped to handle all your oral health needs under one roof. The clinic operates six days a week with flexible morning and evening hours, making quality dental care convenient for working professionals and families alike. Whether you are visiting for the first time or returning for ongoing treatment, you can expect clear communication, meticulous care, and results that make you smile with confidence.

  • ⭐ Rated 4.8/5 by 125+ happy patients — one of Tiruchirappalli's most trusted dental clinics
  • 🦷 In-house Esthetic Dental Lab for faster, high-quality custom dental prosthetics and restorations
  • 🕙 Convenient dual-shift timings (Morning & Evening) six days a week for working professionals and families
